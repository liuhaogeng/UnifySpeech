This page shows the samples in the paper "UnifySpeech: A Unified Framework for Zero-shot Text-to-Speech and Voice Conversion".

Experiments were based on VCTK (use both of the speech and text) and LibriTTS (only use the speech). All test samples have not appeared in the training set and validation set.

The most important advantage of the "UnifySpeech" is that it enables unlabeled data to train TTS indirectly. It also gives a fine-grained and unified representation for speech and phoneme.
